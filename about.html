Hello, I am Jamie Bae

I am a UX/UI Designer with a background in Fine Art and 2D Design. Currently, I’m pursuing a Masters in Entertainment Technology at Carnegie Mellon University in the Greater Pittsburgh. I have worked in inspiring startup companies as a UX/UI designer. I believe that a designer should have great patience to listen, empathize, Synthesize, iterate, story tell and with craftsmanship skills to create an Impactful design. How good is a product which is not just a tool, but serves you like a best friend understanding your needs and surprising in advance. I aspire to collaborate with challenges that allow exploring design strategies, maintain user-centered design methodologies, and who thrive for perfection, to create innovative, usable user interfaces, ensuring high user experience. My goal as a designer is to create engaging, functional and accessible user experiences that delight people and solves complex business problems. In my free time, I enjoy exploring new cuisines, watching diverse films, and reading about design, technology and many other fields.
